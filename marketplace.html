Biodegradable Plates

"Dine sustainably with stubble plates—where nature meets elegance."

Stubble plates combine environmental consciousness with aesthetic appeal, making them a popular choice for eco-conscious consumers and those looking for unique dinnerware options.

Stubble fibers are molded and pressed into plate shapes, then treated to ensure durability and biodegradability.

Bedding for Cattle

"Comfort for your cattle, sustainability for the planet."

Stubble as cattle bedding enhances comfort, reduces waste, and promotes healthier animals. It also contributes to the health of the soil through natural decomposition. This helps to utilise stubble and serves as a noble cause.

Stubble is dried, chopped, and spread as bedding material in cattle shelters.

Carry Bags

"Carry green, carry stubble—eco-friendly bags for a better planet."

Stubble fibers are processed, woven, and stitched into durable carry bags.

Biofuel Production

"Fuel your future with stubble-derived biofuels."

Stubble can be processed into biofuels or used as feedstock for biogas production. Utilizing stubble for biofuel production reduces reliance on fossil fuels and helps mitigate greenhouse gas emissions.

Stubble is collected, processed, and fermented or pyrolyzed to produce biofuels.

Compost & Soil Amendment

"Nourish your soil naturally with stubble compost."

Stubble can be incorporated into compost piles or directly applied to fields as a soil amendment. As it decomposes, it releases nutrients into the soil, improving structure and fertility.

Stubble is chopped into organic waste to create nutrient-rich compost.

Erosion Control

"Protect your soil, preserve your land with stubble erosion control."

Stubble can be left on fields as a protective cover to reduce soil erosion. It helps retain soil moisture and prevent erosion. Stubble helps to check the flow of water in areas of high flow and prevents soil from being displaced.

Stubble is cut and spread over fields or formed into erosion control mats.
